## Context/Memory

### 10-Day Trailing Percentile vs Daily Percentile
- **Daily**: "Is this the best stock TODAY?" (noisy, single-day ranking)
- **Trailing 10-day**: "Has this stock been consistently good over 2 weeks?" (persistent strength)

The trailing percentile ranks each stock's score against ALL scores from the past 10 trading days (including today). This captures momentum/persistence rather than single-day spikes.

### Top N Competition vs Percentile Gate
**Old Approach (Double Gating)**:
1. Percentile gate: Only consider top 5% (`rank >= 0.95`)
2. Regime gate: No entries in Strong Bear

**New Approach (Single Gate)**:
1. Score floor only (`min_score >= 30`) - very permissive
2. Regime controls exposure (position count + sizing)
3. Percentile is just a ranking metric to fill available slots

Result: Regime is the ONLY exposure control. No redundant filtering.

### The -27% Loss Mystery
Hard stop is MAX(2*ATR, 10%), so losses >10% shouldn't happen. Possible causes:
1. **Gap Down**: Stock opens -25% after earnings (stop was 10% below previous close)
2. **Regime Liquidation**: M03 flips to 0, forced market sell into crash
3. **Bug**: Stop not enforced in `position_tracker.py`

The new "Worst Trades" forensic log will identify the specific trade.
