### Hybrid Configuration (ADOPTED)
Combined best of both worlds:
- **Trend: 0.40** (separation driver, keeps Cohen's D high)
- **Risk: 0.40** (speed driver, catches crashes fast)
- **Liquidity: 0.20** (supporting role, fast 10d lookback)
- **VIX: Tight** (Bear=20, Extreme=30)
